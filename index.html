Fascinating Facts About My Monkey Life

Read time: 6 minutes

Hi there! I'm a curious little monkey from the jungles of South America. Life here is pretty exciting, filled with swinging from tree to tree and munching on delicious fruits. Did you know that we monkeys are divided into two big families? We have the New World monkeys, like me, and our cousins, the Old World monkeys, who live in Africa and Asia. We might look alike, but trust me, we're very different in how we live and play.

"Bananas are great, but have you tried a juicy mango?" - Chico the Monkey 2024

I belong to the New World monkeys, and one of my coolest features is my prehensile tail. It's like having an extra hand! I use it to grab onto branches, carry things, and even hang upside down. My Old World cousins, like the baboons, don't have this tail talent, but they're pretty strong and spend a lot of time on the ground.
